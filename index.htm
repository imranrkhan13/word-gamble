Game Rules

1. Players take turns placing letters on the board.

2. The goal is to form valid words using 3 or more letters.

3. Players score points based on the number of letters.

4. Words must not be repeated during gameplay.

5. All submitted words must contain three or more letters.

6. Abbreviations are prohibited.

7. Sequences of repeated letters (e.g., 'AAA') are considered invalid.

8. Only valid words recognized by the Urban dictionary are permitted.
