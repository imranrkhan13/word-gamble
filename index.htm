Game Rules

1. Take turns placing letters on the board.

2. Form valid words (3 or 3+ letters).

3. Score points based on word length.

4. No repeated words allowed.

5. No abbreviations or repeated letter sequences.

6. Only Urban Dictionary words are valid.

7. Game ends when board is full.

8. Highest score wins!

9. Access the word's meaning once it's created.
